Anti-fraud guidelines (summary for testing):

1) Prevent: Use strong KYC, verify beneficiaries, and validate documents before approval.
2) Detect: Monitor transactions, audit trails, anomaly checks, and periodic inspections.
3) Respond: Report suspected fraud quickly, freeze/stop payments, investigate, and recover funds.
4) Governance: Clear roles, maker-checker controls, and training for staff.

Citizen-facing actions: keep receipts, use official portals, never share OTP/PIN, and report fraud to the grievance/helpline.
